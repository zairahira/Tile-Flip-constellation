How to Play

  • Tiles are revealed briefly at the start - memorize their positions.
  • Click any hidden tile to flip it over.
  • Click a second tile to check for a match.
  • Matched pairs stay revealed. Find all pairs to win the level.
  • Some levels have a move limit or a time limit - watch the header.
